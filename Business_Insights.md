# Business Insights

## Executive Summary

This dashboard analyzes the company's financial and operational performance using sales, expenses, inventory, and purchasing data.

## Key Findings

- Total Revenue increased by 18% compared to the previous month.

- The highest selling category was Food Products.

- Inventory turnover improved during Q3.

- Operating expenses represented 34% of total revenue.

- Packaging materials accounted for the highest purchasing cost.

- Three products generated over 45% of total sales.

## Recommendations

- Increase production of high-demand products.

- Reduce purchasing cost for packaging materials.

- Monitor low-profit products.

- Review monthly operating expenses.
